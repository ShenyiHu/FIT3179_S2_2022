Internet Price on World Map

From the map visualisation to the right, it is not hard to tell that internet is no longer a luxury in most of the countries in the world like the old days.

The data is obtained by calculating the average price per gigabyte of all internet plans available for each country, using United States Dollar currency. The grey areas are where data is not available in the data source.

Price difference by continent from 2020 to 2022

The price of internet, despite being affected by the global pandemic during 2020 and 2022, has significantly gone down in every continent due to technology development and data deflation. Have a look at the below graph about the price drop of each continent.

The price difference is calculated by dividing the sum of prices for all countries in the continent by the total number of countries, without considering the number of internet users. This ensure that the data is normalised and not affected by population.
